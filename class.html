Recipe of Aaloo Paratha

Ingredients

  • 1kg Potatoes
  • 1/2kg Wheat flour
  • Salt
  • Turmeric powder
  • Chilly
  • water

Steps to prepare Aaloo Paratha

  1. Boil the potatoes for few minutes.
  2. Take a bowl and put the wheat flour in it and start to make a dough of the flour.
  3. Cut the dough into small pieces so that Chappatti can be made.
  4. Peel up the potatoes and smashed the potatoes.
  5. Mix the salt and turmeric powder in the smashed potatoes and mix well.
  6. Fill the little amout of smashed potatoes in each cut dough.
  7. And then clode the dough and flatten it and make the chappatii of each flattened dough.
  8. Now just put the chappatii on the pan and cook the chappattiii till it gets red while cooking pour some oil on it.
  9. Enjoy the Aaloo Paratha with sauce or chutny as you like.
